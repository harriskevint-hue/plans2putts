Questions

Frequently asked

Can't find your answer? Email us at harriskevint@gmail.com

Which glasses work with ShadesCaddie? +
ShadesCaddie works with Meta Ray-Ban Display glasses ($799) for the full visual HUD, and Oakley Meta HSTN glasses ($479) for voice caddy through the open-ear speakers. Both are available at Best Buy, Meta.com, and Oakley.com.
Do I need my phone on the course? +
Your phone stays in your pocket. ShadesCaddie uses your phone's GPS to calculate distances and delivers them through your glasses. No screen watching required.
How accurate are the yardages? +
Outdoor GPS accuracy is typically 3-5 yards, well within the tolerance needed for club selection. Course data is verified against official scorecards and satellite imagery.
Is my course available? +
The free tier includes your mapped home course. Premium subscribers get access to thousands of courses. If yours isn't in our database, email us — we'll add it.
Is ShadesCaddie an official Meta product? +
ShadesCaddie is an independent app built on Meta's Web Apps platform. We are a registered developer in the Meta Wearables Developer Center. Not affiliated with Meta, Ray-Ban, or Oakley.
