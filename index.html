What ShadesCaddie does

Your caddie is built into your shades

01
Live GPS Yardages

Real-time distance to green front, center, and back. Nearest hazards sorted by proximity. Dogleg and layup callouts — all updated as you walk.

02
Hands-Free Voice Caddy

Yardages spoken through the glasses' open-ear speakers automatically. Hear your distance without touching your phone or looking at a screen.

03
Stroke Counting & Scoring

Mark each shot with a gesture. After every hole: "Hole 7 complete. 5 strokes. Bogey. Total 38 through 7 holes." Golf score vocabulary built in.

04
Downloadable Scorecard

After your round, download a full PDF scorecard with hole-by-hole scores, score vs par, and carry distances per club. Share with your playing partners.

05
Club Distance Tracking

Tag each shot with the club used. Track carry distances every round. Over time ShadesCaddie builds your personal club distance profile.

06
Focus Mode

Display clears automatically before your shot. On the Display glasses, the lens goes fully transparent — nothing in your field of view when you need to concentrate.

Questions

Frequently asked

Which glasses work with ShadesCaddie?
ShadesCaddie works with Meta Ray-Ban Display glasses ($799) for the full visual HUD experience, and with Oakley Meta HSTN glasses ($399) for voice caddy through the built-in speakers. Both are available at Best Buy, Meta.com, and Oakley.com.
Do I need my phone on the course?
Your phone stays in your pocket. ShadesCaddie uses your phone's GPS to calculate distances. For Oakley Meta users, yardages are spoken through the glasses. For Display glasses users, yardages appear on the lens. Either way, no looking at a screen.
How accurate are the yardages?
Outdoor GPS accuracy is typically 3-5 meters (3-5 yards), well within the tolerance needed for club selection. Course data is verified against official scorecards and satellite imagery.
Does it work at my home course?
The free tier includes your home course. Premium subscribers get access to thousands of courses via our course data API. If your course isn't in our database, we can add it — reach out and we'll get it mapped.
Is ShadesCaddie an official Meta product?
ShadesCaddie is an independent developer product built on Meta's Web Apps platform. We are a registered developer in the Meta Wearables Developer Center. Not affiliated with Meta, Ray-Ban, or Oakley.
